Privacy Policy for dapurMamani

Last Updated: 2026

1. Information We Collect and Why

  • Seller Name or Nickname: Used for identification in listings and order reports.
  • Email Address: Used for account verification and password resets.
  • Phone Number: Shared with customers only to coordinate delivery with your consent.
  • Location: Used for mapping food points and delivery routes.
  • Food Details: Name, description, and price are stored for customer display.
  • Preference Analysis: We analyze taste preferences to improve user experience.

2. Required Permissions

  • Camera: For scanning QR codes.
  • Location: For capturing delivery and pickup addresses.
  • Storage: For temporary data and login states.
  • Internet & Notifications: For server synchronization and order alerts.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase, Google Services, OSMDroid, Apache POI, and ZXing.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely in our database. Except for necessary order coordination (e.g., sharing a seller's contact with a buyer), your information will not be shared with any unauthorized third parties.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Data Deletion Rights

Users have the right to request the deletion of their data. If you wish to delete your account or personal information, please contact us at: info.dapurmamani@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
