Privacy Policy for dapurMamani

Last Updated: 2026

1. Information We Collect and Why

  • User Identity: We collect Seller Names/Nicknames and Email Addresses for account verification, order reports, and service notifications.
  • Contact Data:Phone numbers are required for sellers to coordinate food delivery with customers.
  • Location Data: If GPS is enabled, we collect location data to display service points on the map and determine delivery addresses.
  • Content: Food details (names, descriptions, pricing) are stored to be displayed to potential customers.
  • Preference Analysis: We analyze taste preferences to improve user experience.

2. Required Permissions

  • Camera: Used for scanning QR codes for orders.
  • Location: To provide map-based services and delivery tracking.
  • Storage: To store temporary data and export order reports into Excel files.
  • Notifications: To send alerts regarding order status (Android 13+).

3. Third-Party Services

We use reputable third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob:Our app displays advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers to personalize ads.
  • Google Services:For "Login with Google" and Firebase Cloud Messaging.
  • Map Providers:We use OSMDroid along with Esri and CartoDB tiles. These providers may process data to render maps.
  • Data Processing:We use Apache POI for Excel exports and ZXing for QR code functionality.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely. We do not share personal information with unauthorized third parties except for necessary order coordination between buyers and sellers.
